My concern is specifically with the kind of understanding IPSCI is ostensibly (if tacitly) concerned with (i.e. objectual understanding). I will not discuss alethic unification (e.g. Niiniluoto 2018), explanatory unification (e.g.Kitcher, 1989), or the unity of scientific knowledge (e.g.Bird, 2007). Although truth, explanation, and knowledge are, I take it, associated with understanding, I will not go into how such associations may obtain.
